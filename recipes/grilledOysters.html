Grilled Oysters With Spicy Miso Butter

Oyster lover rejoice! This easy grilled oyster recipe with spicy miso butter is sure to please. Don't forget to grab a baguette to soak up all the delicious juices.

Ingreadients.

  • 2 dozen medium to large oysters in the shell.
  • 4 tablespoons unsalter butter, softened to room temperature.
  • 2 teaspoons white miso paste.
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sriracha.
  • 1 large lime, zested.
  • 1 baguette, for serving.
  1. Preheat your grill.
  2. Make the miso butter.
  3. Clean the oysters.
  4. Grill the oysters.
  5. Transfer to a platter and open oysters.
  6. Spoon miso butter onto oysters and return to grill.
  7. Transfer to platter.
  8. SERVE :)
